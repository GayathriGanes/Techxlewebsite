Challenges

Axlerator, our client, faced difficulties managing its sales opportunity pipeline across departments. They lacked visibility into project progress and resource allocation, leading to inefficiencies, missed opportunities, and inconsistent workflows.

Solutions

Axle implemented the APOLLO platform, a custom-built sales CRM solution with the following features:

  • User-Friendly Interface:Built with Power Apps, offering custom forms and views for easy data interaction.
  • Business Process Flows: Structured workflows that guide users through each stage of opportunity management, ensuring consistency.
  • Cloud Flows for Automation: Utilizes Power Automate to automate repetitive tasks, such as record updates and notifications, improving efficiency.

Impact

The refinements to the APOLLO platform aim to enhance transparency and efficiency, reducing manual efforts and streamlining workflows. This is expected to shorten sales cycle times by 20% and improve lead management, ultimately increasing conversion rates.
